What is privacy cafe ?

Privacy is the ability of an individual or group to seclude themselves
or information about themselves, and thereby express themselves selectively.
When something is private to a person, it usually means that something is
inherently special or sensitive to them.

Why we need a privacy Cafe in all town and cities ?

Privacy rights ensure we have control over our data. If it's your data,
you should have control over it. Privacy rights dictate that your data
can only be used in ways you agree to and that you can access any
information about yourself.
